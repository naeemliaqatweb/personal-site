AsboEstates.com

I developed AsboEstates.com, a Yii2-based real estate platform specializing in property sales and rentals. The project encompassed various advanced features to enhance user experience and functionality.

  • Property Search Functionality: Implemented a robust search system allowing users to filter properties by type, location, price range, and more.
  • Google Maps Integration: Integrated Google Maps to display property locations, providing users with geographical context and aiding in decision-making.
  • Multi-Language Support: Developed the platform to support multiple languages, catering to a diverse international clientele.
  • Blog Section: Created a blog to share insights, news, and updates related to the real estate market and the region.
  • Inquiry Form: Designed and integrated an inquiry form to facilitate direct communication between potential buyers or renters and the agency.
  • API Integration: Leveraged Yii2's capabilities to integrate with external services via RESTful APIs, enhancing the platform's functionality and connectivity.
