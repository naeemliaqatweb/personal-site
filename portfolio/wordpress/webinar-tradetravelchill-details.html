Webinar.TradeTravelChill.Club

I developed Webinar.TradeTravelChill.Club from scratch, a WordPress-based LMS platform designed for structured online learning.

  • MS LMS Plugin & Stripe Integration: Implemented MS LMS for course management and integrated Stripe for seamless payment processing.
  • Dual Login System: Users can register either via UID & exchange selection or opt for a $47 paid registration to access courses.
  • Course Progression & Custom Video Delivery: Lessons unlock sequentially, ensuring structured learning. Upon 100% video completion, a custom-generated video is sent to the user.
  • Elementor-Based Design: Designed a professional homepage using Elementor and customized lesson pages for an enhanced user experience.
  • Membership & Content Restriction: Implemented membership controls, ensuring that only registered users can access the content.
