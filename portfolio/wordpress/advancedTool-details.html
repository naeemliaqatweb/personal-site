AdvancedTool.com

I developed AdvancedTool.com from scratch, a WooCommerce-powered eCommerce website specializing in drill sales.

  • WooCommerce Integration: Built a fully functional online store with WooCommerce for selling drills.
  • Payment Gateway Setup: Integrated Authorize.Net and Cash on Delivery (COD) payment options.
  • Multiple Drill Categories: Created and organized various product categories for easy navigation.
  • Elementor Pro Customization: Designed and developed the entire website using Elementor Pro for a visually appealing and user-friendly interface.
